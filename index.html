About me

Hi there! My name is Tracy, born and raised in Manila (Philippines). An avid traveller and a frustrated artist. I had spent a big chunk of my working career in Singapore. I've since then transitioned to a digital nomad lifestyle and is now based in Limassol (Cyprus). I enjoy creating stuff in general. I'm in love with the fusion of technology and the arts. I've had a bittersweet relationship with programming and web development. Back in college, I took a degree in Computer Studies specializing in Information Management. I struggled a lot at that time and vowed to never pursue it as a career. Fast forward to 17 years later, I have rekindled my passion for coding through SheCodes. And now here I am, enjoying every single moment of it.
